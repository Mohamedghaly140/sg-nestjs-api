## Summary

All `CODING_STANDARDS.md` §6 bullets are verified as implemented (not just documented). Two real gaps were found and fixed: the coupon-validate throttle override and the webhook throttle policy (Workstream 1), and 3 transitive dependency vulnerabilities (this workstream). One item (#3, card data) is structurally moot until Phase 7 ships, since CARD checkout is now rejected outright. No CSP/helmet configuration change was needed — verified rather than assumed.
